Our Mission

The DeepFake Detection project was created to address the growing challenge of manipulated media content in our digital ecosystem. As deepfake technology becomes more sophisticated and accessible, we recognized the urgent need for reliable detection methods to preserve trust in digital content.

Our mission is to develop and provide an easy-to-use yet powerful solution that can identify manipulated media with high accuracy, helping users distinguish between authentic and synthetic content. We believe in the power of technology to solve problems that technology itself creates.

Key Objectives:

  • To advance deepfake detection research through innovative techniques
  • To create accessible tools for both technical and non-technical users
  • To raise awareness about the capabilities and limitations of deepfake technology
  • To contribute to a more secure and trustworthy digital media landscape

Technology Stack

Our DeepFake Detection System leverages cutting-edge technologies in machine learning, computer vision, and web development to deliver a comprehensive solution:

AI & Machine Learning

  • PyTorch for deep learning models
  • ResNet-50 architecture for feature extraction
  • LIME for explainable AI visualizations
  • Spatial-temporal analysis techniques

Web Development

  • Flask web framework for backend
  • Bootstrap 5 for responsive UI
  • Chart.js for interactive visualizations
  • HTML5 and modern CSS features

Our system processes images in just 12.7ms on average, enabling real-time detection with over 90% accuracy across multiple datasets. The user interface is designed to be intuitive while providing detailed insights through our explainable AI component.
